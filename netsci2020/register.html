How to Apply

Interested participants may email a one page note to the email netsci2020@uohyd.ac.in indicating why he/she wants to attend the programme. The email should be attached with the filled application form that can be downloaded from here on or before 20 February 2020.

Selected applicants will be informed by email, and those selected should register for the programme by paying the Registration fees through Electronic Fund Transfer by 10 March 2020. Details will be provided to the selected participants by email.

Please remember the following dates:

Intimation of selected applicants : 21 February 2020

Payment of registration fees : 10 March 2020
